About Me

Hello, my name is Tristan Carter. As far back as I can remember, I loved taking things apart. If something was broken, I wanted to see what broke and how to fix it. After so much taking apart, I flipped the switch and decided to construct instead of destruct. From building my own furniture to full on websites, my process of taking things apart and putting them back together leaves room to learn the best practices on: seperation of concerns, specific functionality, and looking at the big picture from little pieces.

I like to code custom solutions for small to medium businesses to get their big picture in front of the right eyes. Every business starts small but we belive that the right team with the right tools can carve out their own share of the market.
